Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

GullyScore ("we", "our", or "the app") is a gully cricket scoring application for iOS and Apple Watch. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

When you use GullyScore, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from your Google account. This is used solely for authentication and displaying your identity within groups.
  • Cricket data: Match scores, player names, team compositions, and match statistics that you create within the app.
  • Group data: Group names, membership lists, and group codes that you create or join.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to provide the app's functionality:

  • Authenticate your identity via Google Sign-In
  • Sync your match data, player stats, and groups across your devices
  • Enable live match sharing with other group members
  • Link your account to a player identity within groups

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in two places:

  • On your device: Match data, player data, and group data are stored locally on your iPhone using standard iOS storage.
  • In the cloud: When signed in, your data is synced to Google Firebase (Firestore) to enable cross-device sync and live match sharing. Firebase servers are operated by Google and are subject to Google's privacy practices.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your cricket data is only visible to members of groups you belong to. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

GullyScore uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In: For authentication (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Firebase Authentication: For secure user management
  • Firebase Firestore: For cloud data storage and real-time sync

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Unlink your identity from any player in a group
  • Sign out at any time, which stops cloud syncing

To request data deletion, contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

GullyScore is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
