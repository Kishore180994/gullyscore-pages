Delete Your Account

Last updated: April 19, 2026

You can delete your GullyScore account and the personal data associated with it at any time. This page explains exactly what happens when you do.

How to request deletion

Send an email to the address below from the email account you use to sign in to GullyScore. Include the words "Delete my GullyScore account" in the subject line.

Email a deletion request

Your request will be processed within 7 days. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

What gets deleted

Within 7 days of confirming your request, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your account profile (display name, email address, profile photo URL)
  • Your Firebase Authentication record (so you can no longer sign in with that account)
  • Your group memberships
  • Player identity links (any player profiles you were linked to in groups)
  • Any matches where you were the only group member

What is kept (and why)

The following data may be retained in anonymized form for the integrity of shared group history:

  • Matches you scored or played in alongside others: the match record (scores, balls, wickets, results) is kept so the rest of the group does not lose their match history. Your name will be removed and replaced with a generic placeholder (e.g. Deleted Player).
  • Group records you created: if other members are still using a group you created, the group is preserved and ownership transfers to the next admin. Your name is removed from the member list.
  • Aggregated, anonymous statistics with no personal identifiers.

If you would prefer all match-history references to also be deleted (including those visible to other group members), say so in your email and we will accommodate where possible.

What about cached data on my device?

Uninstalling GullyScore from your phone removes the local cache. Account deletion above only covers data stored on our servers (Firebase). To remove local data without uninstalling: in the app, sign out, then clear app storage from your device settings.
